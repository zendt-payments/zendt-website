(01) · How it works Four steps · Two minutes

Four steps. Zero friction.

No bank visits. No SWIFT forms. No paperwork. Just a link, a payment, and a settlement straight into your Indian bank account.

STEP 01

Create & send an invoice

Build a polished invoice in seconds. The payment link is generated automatically and built right in. Works for domestic and international clients alike.

STEP 02

Share your Zendt payment link

One link works for every client, every currency. Send it via WhatsApp, email, or attached to your invoice.

STEP 03

Client pays in their currency

Your client sees a clean checkout in their local currency: USD, AED, GBP, AUD, CAD, and 145 more. UPI, card, or bank.

STEP 04

Money hits your account in INR

Funds settle in your Indian bank account at live FX. T+3 for international, T+1 for domestic. We handle the compliance.

(05) · Origin Built by a freelancer, for freelancers

A NOTE FROM THE FOUNDER

"I spent five years freelancing for clients in the GCC, UK, US, Canada and beyond. I watched a third of my income disappear into fees, FX markups, and bank transfers that took weeks. So we're building the product I wished existed."

Zendt isn't a payments dashboard built by people who read about freelancing. It is the tool I needed at 2 AM, chasing a wire transfer that had stalled at a correspondent bank somewhere in London. Every decision, from the 4% flat rate to the GCC corridor support to the settlement speed, comes from a real invoice that cost me real money.

Alen Thomas Founder, Zendt Payments
