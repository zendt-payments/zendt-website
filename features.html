Product

Built for the work — not just the payout.

Zendt is the toolkit freelancers use to run their business. Client work comes first; getting paid is one feature among many.

01

Invoicing

Polished invoices with your branding, automatic reminders, and reconciliation so you always know what's outstanding.

02

Client management

A light CRM: store contacts, see history at a glance, and keep notes for every client relationship.

03

Projects & milestones

Track deliverables, deadlines, and project value. Know exactly where each engagement stands.

04

Earnings dashboard

See income by client, currency, and month. Filter, forecast, and export for your CA.

05

Freelancer profile

A public portfolio page with your services and a way for clients to reach you — one link to share.

Accept 150+ fiat currencies and domestic UPI. Handled by Zwitch and RBI-licensed banking partners — not by Zendt.
