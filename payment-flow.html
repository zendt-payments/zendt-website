What we do

Zendt Payments is a freelancer toolkit built for Indian freelancers and independent professionals, powered by RBI-regulated banking and payment partners. We provide invoicing, hosted payment links, and a single dashboard to manage domestic client payments, with INR settlement to your Indian savings account.

Who we onboard: Indian freelancers, consultants, designers, developers, educators, and other independent professionals who earn from clients in India and abroad, across software & IT, creative services, management consulting, and education & training.
