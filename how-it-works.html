How it works

Your work, organised — from client to invoice to payout.

Four steps framed around running your freelance business. Zendt is the software; licensed partners handle money movement.

01

Add clients & set up your profile

Import contacts, build your public freelancer page, and keep notes on every relationship in one place.

02

Create & send invoices

Build professional invoices in seconds. A payment link is built in — ready for international or domestic clients.

03

Track projects & milestones

Follow deliverables, see what's outstanding, and know what each engagement is worth without a spreadsheet.

04

Get paid to your INR account

Clients pay in their currency or via UPI. Settlement to your Indian bank account is handled by licensed partners (T+3 international, T+1 domestic).
