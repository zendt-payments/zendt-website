What's included in the toolkit

Everything you need to run client work — invoicing, a light CRM, project tracking, earnings views, and a shareable profile. These are software features, not payment services.

How payment fees work

When a client pays through a link on your invoice, licensed partners handle collection and INR settlement. Zendt charges a platform fee on successful transactions: 4% for international receipts, 0.6% for UPI. GST applies on the fee only.

Common questions

  • Is there a monthly subscription? No. The toolkit is free to start; you pay per successful payment.
  • Who handles settlement? RBI-licensed banking partners — not Zendt.
  • Are there hidden FX charges? No markup on mid-market rates for international receipts.
